RUDM 2025 Merch Designs
I am a Marketing Captain for Rutgers University Dance Marathon, the largest philanthropic organization on campus. Throughout the year, our team fundraises for the Embrace Kids Foundation, which provides essential support for pediatric patients and their families, including counseling, transportation, family services, and financial assistance during times of need. At the end of the year, we organize a large-scale event celebrating our fundraising efforts, culminating in the reveal of the total amount raised and drawing over a thousand attendees. I was responsible for designing event merchandise and the official event t-shirts, which were worn by all staff and participants.

Livia Foldes Invitation Poster

Using the visual metaphor of censorship, I created an engaging poster design for a visiting artist lecturer, Livia Foldes. Foldes’ most profound work was her MFA thesis entitled “Sex Workers Built the Internet.” Through her research and inquiry, she attempts to amplify the experiences of sex workers, dismantling stigmatized perceptions of sex work, noting that the community has been dehumanized, sanitized, and erased from a platform they built: the internet.
